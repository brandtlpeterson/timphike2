About Me

I’m a dependable worker with strong communication and problem-solving skills. I’ve worked in customer-facing, hands-on, and teaching roles, which has helped me become adaptable and comfortable in fast-paced environments.

From working as a cashier and carpenter to managing warehouse logistics and teaching at the Missionary Training Center, I’ve learned how to collaborate with teams, stay organized, and keep a positive attitude.

Outside of work, I enjoy hiking Utah’s mountains, learning languages, and finding ways to serve and uplift others.

Why Mt. Timpanogos?

Mt. Timpanogos is one of Utah Valley’s most iconic peaks. The trail offers wildflowers, ridgelines, and sweeping views over Utah Lake and the surrounding mountains.

Preparation Steps for a Long Hike

Here’s a simple guide to getting ready for a full-day hike on Timp:

  1. Plan Your Route
    • Choose a trailhead (Timpooneke or Aspen Grove).
    • Check recent trail conditions and weather.
    • Set a realistic turnaround time.
  2. Pack Essentials
    • Water (and a way to filter more).
    • Food and snacks for the whole day.
    • Layers, rain jacket, and sun protection.
  3. Prepare Your Body
    • Get enough sleep the night before.
    • Start early to avoid afternoon storms.
    • Warm up and pace yourself on the first miles.
